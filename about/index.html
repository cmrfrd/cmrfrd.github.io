What is The Art of Abstraction?

The Art of Abstraction is a tech blog with the goal to educate and demonstrate the various methods of abstracting complexity. It was started with the purpose to become an exhibition for provocative ideas and insightful projects in subfields of engineering and science.

Every post is made to provide value to experts and beginners alike. Whether coming from a tech background or not, the metaphors in each post are meant to evoke a sense of freedom and delight towards the intricate.
